 Analytics frontiers and other conferences give an opportunity for analytics experts and specialists take a moment to step back, reflect on the work they’re doing and also learn from some of the brightest minds in the industries that they’re trying to learn more about. It’s very important for UNC Charlotte to create a venue for discussing important issues around data science and the analytics frontiers conference is such a venue. So its a venue for identifying opportunities around data science, the research issues around data science, the talent needs of Charlotte and North Carolina in data science. The conference allows the university to showcase what is possible through practical applications. Its a blend of what academic knowledge the university generates, the students, the training that they’re provided, as well as how business in the community are using these kind of approaches to solve different kind of problems that exist within the business and society at large.
