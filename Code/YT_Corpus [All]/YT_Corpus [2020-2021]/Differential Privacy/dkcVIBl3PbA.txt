 Hello everyone Welcome to our preview talk The provably security of Ed25519: theory and practice Nowadays, one of the most popular digital signature schemes is EdDSA By virtue of high efficiency, the Ed25519 instantiation has been widely deployed in various practical protocols However, it turns out the implementations differ substantially and the devil is in the detail Among them, three of the most significant ones are the original Ed25519 and its IETF and Libsodium variants Suprisingly, no detailed security analysis have ever been given for them In our paper, we provide the first detailed analysis of the provable security of these three implementations If you are interested in our conclusions, please read our paper and come to our talk
