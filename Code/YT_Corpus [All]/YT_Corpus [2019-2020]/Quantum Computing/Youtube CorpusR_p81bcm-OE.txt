 Hello and welcome to the Institute for Quantum Computing at the University of Waterloo. Take a look around, right now you're standing in the lobby of an institution that has led quantum information research for more than 15 years. Here at IQC, state-of-the-art laboratories house world-renowned researchers who are focused on four core areas: quantum computing, quantum communication, quantum materials and quantum sensors. Throughout these halls and spiraling staircases quantum information is being explored through experiments in optics, nano electronics, nuclear magnetic resonance, and a lot more. Waterloo's unique research capacity, commercialization support, and exciting cluster of quantum technology businesses, offering products in quantum software communication and security have led the community to be known globally as "Quantum Valley".
