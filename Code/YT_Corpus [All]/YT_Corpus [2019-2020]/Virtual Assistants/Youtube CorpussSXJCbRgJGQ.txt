 Today we are providing a quick tutorial on how to make an appointment for virtual assistance. The first step is to go to library.tamu.edu. Click on the "Services" button, then go to the "Technology" tab, and click on Studio. Click on "Virtual Consultations" to book an appointment. Select your preferred meeting time. Each box is 30 minutes. Click on the drop-down menu below to extend your time. Click "Submit Times." Enter your Net ID enter your password and click "Continue." Enter the required information and submit your booking. Once completed you have the option to make another booking, or simply log out. Any Questions?
