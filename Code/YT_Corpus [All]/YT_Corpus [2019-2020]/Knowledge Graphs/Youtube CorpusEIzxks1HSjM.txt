 Use EquatIO prediction terms right inside the Desmos Graph Editor. Some of the most common types of exponents you can use are: ‘to the power’, ‘squared’, ‘cubed’, ‘superscript’, or ‘nth power’. Notice that as you type in your expressions your Graph Editor will express the solution off to the right side of the box. Commonly used roots you may find in graphing include ‘square root’, ‘nth root’, or cube root.
