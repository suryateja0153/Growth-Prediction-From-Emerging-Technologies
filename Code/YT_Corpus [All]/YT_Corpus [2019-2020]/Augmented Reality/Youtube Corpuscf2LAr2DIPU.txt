 tpl:ar is the new Toronto Public Library augmented reality app. Use it to discover hidden content by scanning select Toronto Public Library items and publications. With the app you have access to hidden digital content such as animations, pictures and videos. tpl:ar is easy to use. Once you download the app just open it, grant it permission to use your camera, look at your screen and then point your camera at one of Toronto Public Library's augmented reality enabled posters, brochures, magazines or other items. Visit tpl.ca/ar and download the app today.
