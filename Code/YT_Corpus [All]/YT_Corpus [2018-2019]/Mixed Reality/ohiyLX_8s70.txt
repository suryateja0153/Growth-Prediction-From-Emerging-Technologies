 MixCast is the best way to create and share mixed reality content in any environment in almost any VR app. This short guide will walk you through your first time setup of MixCast and have you ready to create real-time MR content in three easy steps. Step 1: Select your input device Step 2: Remove your background Step 3: Align your camera Before we get started, make sure your VR setup is complete and any cameras are connected and on. MixCast supports the Oculus Rift, Windows Mixed Reality headsets, and the HTC Vive, which we'll be using in this guide After successfully installing MixCast, launch Configuration to begin mixed reality setup.
