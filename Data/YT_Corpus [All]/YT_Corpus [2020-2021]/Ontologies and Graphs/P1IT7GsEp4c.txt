 We will create a discrete mapping for nodes shape and color based on the node's "TYPE" attribute Click the "Style" tab in the Control Panel... In the available Properties, let's choose "Fill Color" and expand the cell As Mapping Type, choose "Discrete Mapping"... ...then as Column, choose "TYPE" Among the available values, double click "Protein" and pick the RED color Now expand the "Shape" cell Again, choose "Discrete Mapping" as Mapping Type and choose "TYPE" as Column Among the available values, double click "Protein"... ... select the Diamond shape and confirm Your network will now have all protein nodes displayed as red diamonds while all other nodes maintain default color and shape
