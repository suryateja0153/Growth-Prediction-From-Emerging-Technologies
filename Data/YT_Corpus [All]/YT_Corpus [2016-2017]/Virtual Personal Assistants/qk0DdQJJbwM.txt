 Meet Alfred the virtual assistant to help you in many daily tasks. When you execute a task, Alfred will follow you along and learn it so he can execute it again in the future, if you desire. As the times pass, Alfred will be learning more and more leaving you free to make your day more productive. Even your social network interactions can benefit from Alfred and publish the content the way you want! Alfred knows a task that could be useful for a friend. You can share tasks with other users and help grow the community while you discover new ways to optimize your day! Alfred understands your commands in English, Portuguese and other languages running on the Google Chrome browser in devices connected with the internet. Visit caosoft.com to learn more.
